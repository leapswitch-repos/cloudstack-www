Upcoming CloudStack Events

There's always plenty of Cloudstack Events happening in different parts of the world. These range from Meetups, User Groups to CloudStack Collaboration Conference events. Each event will feature a lot of opportunities to learn, collaborate and engage with the CloudStack community. The CloudStack Collaboration Conferences are valuable for both developers and users, and are a great way to get involved with the community.

  • Meet Apache CloudStack at CloudFest 2023, Europa-Park Germany, March 21-23 2023
  • CloudStack Collaboration Conference 2022 (Hybrid), Hybrid Event, November 14-16 2022
  • CloudStack European User Group (vCSEUG), Virtual Event, 7th April 2022
  • CloudStack Collaboration Conference 2021, Virtual Event, November 8-12 2021
  • CloudStack European User Group (vCSEUG), Virtual Event, 27th May 2021
  • CloudStack European User Group (vCSEUG) roundup, Virtual Event, 27th February 2020
  • CloudStack European User Group meetup, Berlin, 27th February 2020
  • CloudStack European User Group & Ceph Day, London, 24th October 2019
  • CloudStack Collaboration Conference North America, Las Vegas, 9th - 11th September 2019
  • CloudStack European User Group meetup, Sofia, 13th June 2019
